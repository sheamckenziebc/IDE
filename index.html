About Loyalty6

Delivering excellence since 2017

Loyalty 6 Energy Services is a locally owned and operated Oil and Gas Service company based in Dawson Creek, British Columbia. Founded in 2017, we take pride in delivering top quality pipefitting services from our highly skilled team committed to fueling success with integrity, pride, loyalty, and unmatched quality.

Our expertise spans a wide range of services, including construction, decommissioning, maintenance, pipeline layout, and comprehensive turnaround work and planning. At Loyalty 6 Energy Services, we fuel success by exceeding expectations with a focus on professionalism, precision, and dedication.
